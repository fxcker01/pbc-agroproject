DESIGNING

Design of construction objects is one of the most important directions of the PBC-Agroprojekt company. We perform the entire complex of works, from a detailed study of the site for the object to the development of the necessary documents. It does not matter if the design process has already started or not. After all, our specialists will be able to participate in the design work at any stage, so that the customer receives a high-quality project, because the final result depends on this - the high-quality construction of the object. There are no small or big projects for us. The most important thing for us is to find a rational solution for the construction of the object together with the customer. For each of the customers, our company provides an individual solution.

PBC-AGROPROJEKT PERFORMS THE FOLLOWING TYPES OF SERVICES

On the basis of surveying the relief of the surrounding territory, road construction, engineering networks, and other building elements, we conduct an analysis of topographical conditions for the start of construction.

(including static sounding) is an important design stage that allows you to determine the economic feasibility of construction in a given area. In the process of work, the physical and mechanical properties of the soil, features of the relief are studied in detail, possible risks that may arise in the process of construction work are determined.

Based on the data of the previous stages, we develop planning, architectural and construction, engineering solutions for the project. We provide the Customer with a general plan of the object for consideration, which includes a comprehensive description of the object: dimensions of the premises, level markings, number of floors, sections of the building and other details.

We elaborate in detail the working drawings of the construction structures of the object.

Includes layout design and installation of utilities. We thoroughly work out the planning details in order to ensure comfortable operating conditions of these systems after the completion of construction works.

The drawings are developed in strict compliance with regulatory and technical documents, current norms, which guarantees the safety of further operation of electrical equipment. We provide the Customer with solutions for the organization, installation of cable lines, internal and external electric lighting in accordance with current regulations.

It is the basis of fire safety of the building. Based on these planning decisions, we provide the Customer with schemes for the most optimal location of fire alarm systems. The project is being developed in accordance with regulatory and technical documentation.

A special section, which includes the analysis of the impact of the construction object on the surrounding area. Starting from December 2017, according to the Law of Ukraine "On Environmental Impact Assessment" (EIA), it is prohibited to start economic activity without conducting an environmental impact assessment and obtaining a decision on the planned activity. We analyze the quantitative indicators of the impact of the planned activity on the environment, predict the degree of impact and develop measures to prevent harmful action or minimize it. "PBС-Agroprojekt" carries out design and coordination of environmental impact assessment, both as a section of a comprehensive work project and separately.
