CHERKASIS COMPANY HAS DEVELOPED AN UNIQUE CONCEPT OF CONSTRUCTION OF HOUSING FOR FORCED DISPLACEMENTS

HELPING MANUFACTURERS: THE CONSTRUCTION OF MULTIFUNCTIONAL HANGARS WAS OPERATED BY THE CHERKASY DEVELOPER

A universal solution for any industry. Cherkasy design and construction company "PBC-Agroprojekt" launched the design and construction of multifunctional hangars made of precast reinforced concrete. The special feature of the hangar model is high quality, durability and speed of installation. This is reported on the company's Facebook page. The company's specialists are convinced that due to its convenience and versatility, this model is in the greatest demand among manufacturers and businesses, especially in today's conditions, when it is necessary to save time and money. The construction technology was explained by Oleksandr Pokhodenko, director of PBC-StroyTrade: "We borrowed the idea of creating such a hangar from Soviet times, but modernized and improved it. Metal structures were replaced with precast concrete structures. So, a precast concrete hangar consists of foundation cups, where half-frames are installed - structures made of precast concrete, which have a shape reminiscent of a hockey stick. The width of the hangar is 21 m, the length can be any, depending on the needs of the customer. However, if necessary, we can assemble the object from other reinforced concrete structures". According to Oleksandr Pokhodenko, the hangar belongs to capital construction. One square meter of the object costs from 3,000 hryvnias. The price includes the manufacture of precast concrete structures (foundation cups, reinforced concrete frame, wall panels, floor slabs), their assembly/installation and wall insulation. Determining the cost of foundations is possible after soil geology is performed.
